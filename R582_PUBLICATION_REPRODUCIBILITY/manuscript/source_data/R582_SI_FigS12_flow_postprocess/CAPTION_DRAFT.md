**Supplementary Fig. S12 | Conditional flow dependence of the analytical
boundary-layer scenario.** The registered baseline terms were postprocessed
with $\delta/\delta_0=(v/v_0)^{-m}$ for $m=0.4$, 0.5 and 0.6, with
$\delta_0=25\ \mu$m at the nominal 50 mL min$^{-1}$ flow. All three curves
therefore pass through the registered average-saturation point
$Q_s=83.020$ mAh cm$^{-2}$ at the nominal flow. The shaded band marks the
declared 25–100 mL min$^{-1}$ range; over this interval the $m=0.5$ scenario
moves $Q_s$ from 74.992 to 88.733 mAh cm$^{-2}$. The curves connect registered
analytical evaluations and rescale only the sub-grid generation contribution;
they are not a full flow-dependent boundary-layer solve or a measured
mass-transfer law (`E-POST`).
