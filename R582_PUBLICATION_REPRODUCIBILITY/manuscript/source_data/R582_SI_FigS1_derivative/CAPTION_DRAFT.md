# Supplementary Figure S1 caption

**Supplementary Fig. S1 | Derivative-window sensitivity within one physical
cell.** **a–d**, Full-cell charge-curve derivatives for the four eligible
sequential cycles (cycles 20–23) of the same pristine 1 M ZnI2 + 4 M NH4Br
cell. Each trace was interpolated to a 0.25 mAh cm−2 grid and differentiated
with a cubic Savitzky–Golay estimator using the three prespecified physical
windows shown by the direct line labels: 5.25, 10.25 (primary) and 15.25 mAh
cm−2. Open circles mark the registered rise onset, defined from the 20–50 mAh
cm−2 baseline as the first point at Q ≥ 50 mAh cm−2 above the median plus four
median absolute deviations for five consecutive grid points. Filled triangles
mark the registered maximum within 50 mAh cm−2 to Qend − 2.5 mAh cm−2.
Boundary-derivative fluctuations outside that maximum-search interval remain
visible and are not relabelled as features. All panels use the same derivative
scale. The four cycles are repeated measures within one physical cell, not
independent replicates; the display therefore reports estimator and
within-cell cycle sensitivity without a population interval or inferential
test. These full-cell features do not identify a positive-electrode internal
state, and no model marker was fitted to them. Derived electrolyte labels use
NH4Br under `EXP-META-001`; raw records are unchanged.
