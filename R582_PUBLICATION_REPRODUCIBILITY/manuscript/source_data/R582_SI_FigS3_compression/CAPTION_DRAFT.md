# Supplementary Figure S3 caption

**Supplementary Fig. S3 | Protocol-bounded capacity brackets across existing
felt-thickness cells.** **a**, Primary endpoint at a within-cell median
coulombic-efficiency threshold of 85%. Each lower marker is the highest tested
command whose median coulombic efficiency met the threshold; the capped upper
end is the first higher observed command that failed it. Thus [pass, fail) is
an observed program bracket, not a confidence interval. The blue line connects
only the 1.5, 2.5 and 3.0 mm explicit-thickness cells acquired in March 2025.
The open 2.0 mm diamond is an implicit-standard-thickness cell from a different
acquisition batch and is deliberately unconnected. **b**, Sensitivity of the
last-passing command to prespecified 80%, 85% and 90% median-efficiency
thresholds. The ordering across the three explicit-thickness cells is retained;
the 2.5 mm last-passing command changes at the 90% definition. The 2.0 mm proxy
remains 100 mAh cm−2 for all three definitions and remains separate. Each
thickness is represented by one physical cell, so the records provide
descriptive protocol brackets rather than a population scaling law. Nominal
installed felt thickness is the recorded descriptor; batch equivalence, pore
volume and morphology are not inferred. Derived electrolyte labels use NH4Br
under `EXP-META-001`; raw records are unchanged.
