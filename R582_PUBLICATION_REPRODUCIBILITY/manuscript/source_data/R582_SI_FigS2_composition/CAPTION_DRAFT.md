# Supplementary Figure S2 caption

**Supplementary Fig. S2 | Cell-level descriptive capacity envelope across the
existing NH4Br composition records.** Each filled marker is one physical cell
after conservative continuation stitching and gives the maximum, across
commanded levels, of the within-level median delivered areal capacity. The
vertical segment spans the full sequential-cycle range at that selected command
level, and the short black bar gives the median across physical cells at the
same composition. The displayed cell counts are 1/1/1/2/1 at 0/1/2/3/4 M
NH4Br. The two acquisitions retained at 2 M form one conservatively stitched
physical cell, whereas the two 3 M records are distinct cells; one of the two
3 M endpoint determinations is right-censored. Sequential cycles are repeated
measurements within a cell and do not supply independent replicates. The ranges
are therefore descriptive, not population confidence intervals, and no fitted
concentration law, optimum or plateau is inferred. These are full-cell records
and do not identify a positive-electrode internal state. Derived electrolyte
labels use NH4Br under `EXP-META-001`; raw records are unchanged.
