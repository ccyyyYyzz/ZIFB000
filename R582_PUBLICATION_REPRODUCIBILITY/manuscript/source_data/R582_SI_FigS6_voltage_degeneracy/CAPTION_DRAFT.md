**Supplementary Fig. S6 | Full-cell voltage fit and
accessibility–coefficient degeneracy.** **a,** Selected existing pristine
full-cell charge trace and reduced observation-layer fit. The shaded interval
contains the 72 selected points from 2.672 to 97.540 mAh cm$^{-2}$ used in
$V=c_0+c_1Q+b_{\rm eff}\ln(1/R_{\theta})$; the lower axis shows fit minus
data. The
fit gives $c_0=1.33554$ V, $c_1=9.819\times10^{-4}$ V per
(mAh cm$^{-2}$), $b_{\rm eff}=0.13622$ V and an RMSE of 7.24 mV.
**b,** End reduced accessibility factor
$R_{\theta,\mathrm{end}}=1-\theta_{\mathrm{end}}$ and effective-coefficient
pairs that preserve the selected 70.813 mV voltage contribution; open circles
are the registered R538 pairs and the line is the corresponding analytical
relation. $R_{\theta}$ is a reduced, calibration-controlled factor and is not
the native remaining-area fraction, for which
$A_{\mathrm{bare}}/A_0=R_{\theta}T_{\mathrm{pore}}$. The reduced-factor
trajectory supplies the fit shape. The result is an identifiability diagnostic,
not independent positive-electrode validation or a deposit-morphology
determination.
