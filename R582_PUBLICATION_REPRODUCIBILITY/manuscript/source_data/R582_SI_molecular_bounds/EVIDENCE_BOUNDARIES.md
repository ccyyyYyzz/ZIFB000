# Evidence boundaries for Figures S7-S9

All three figures are `E-COMP` evidence.  They supply bounded molecular
placement or mobility priors for the porous ZIFB positive-electrode model.
They do not independently validate the continuum accessibility relation.

- S7 reports finite-periodic-cell electronic energies and exact optimized
  geometries.  It does not report solution free energies, site populations,
  adsorption equilibrium constants, or nucleation barriers.  The unavailable
  raw single-I2 CDD cubes are not used.
- S8 reports the registered compact-minus-separated electronic-energy
  diagnostic, exact optimized geometries and the recoverable true two-I2 CDD
  grid.  The CDD is a field diagnostic, not a reaction coordinate.  No
  association pathway or morphology is inferred.
- S9 reports bulk self-diffusivities for one trajectory at each of five SOC
  compositions under each of two charge parameterizations.  Block statistics
  describe within-trajectory stability, not independent-replica uncertainty.
  Absolute values remain force-field dependent and require porosity/tortuosity
  treatment before any use as a continuum effective-diffusivity prior.
