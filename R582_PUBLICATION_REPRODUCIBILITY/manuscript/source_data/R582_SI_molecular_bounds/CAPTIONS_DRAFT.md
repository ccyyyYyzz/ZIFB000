# R582 SI molecular-figure captions

**Figure S7 | Relative single-I2 adsorption-energy ordering and optimized
geometries.** **a,** BSSE-corrected periodic adsorption energies expressed
relative to basal carbon.  The tested C-OH and C=O motifs are lower by 0.595
and 0.446 eV, respectively, whereas the tested vacancy is 0.084 eV above the
basal reference. **b-e,** Orthographic local views of the corresponding
optimized XYZ structures in the same order (C-OH, C=O, basal and vacancy).
Coordinates are unchanged; only a disclosed local crop and fixed projection
are applied. Lines between atoms are distance-based display adjacency guides,
not bond-order calculations. The values compare adsorption energies within
the declared finite periodic calculations; they are not solution free
energies, adsorption populations or nucleation barriers. Single-I2
charge-density-difference fields are not shown because the raw cubes are not
available in the registered package.

**Figure S8 | Compact and separated two-I2 configurations at the tested C-OH
site.** **a,** Electronic energy relative to the separated reference, with
`Delta E = E_compact - E_separated = -0.689 eV`. **b,c,** Orthographic local
views of the exact optimized compact and separated XYZ structures; coordinates
are unchanged and the I-I pair assignment minimizes the summed intramolecular
distance. **d,** Recoverable true charge-density-difference grid for the
compact configuration, displayed as the original grid points satisfying
`delta rho >= +0.002 e A^-3` (blue circles, accumulation) or
`delta rho <= -0.002 e A^-3` (amber triangles, depletion). Values are not
smoothed or interpolated; periodic x/y translations only place each retained
grid point beside the unwrapped compact cluster. The CDD is a field diagnostic,
not a reaction coordinate. The energy difference does not define a solution
association free energy, equilibrium constant, nucleation pathway or kinetic
barrier.

**Figure S9 | Full bulk-MD carrier-mobility ladder under two charge
parameterizations.** **a,b,** Species-resolved self-diffusivities for the
charge-scaled `q = 0.8` ECC and formal-charge `q = 1.0` parameterizations,
respectively, at the condition stated above the panels. Markers are means over
the five SOC compositions. Thin horizontal spans are the minimum-to-maximum
range across those compositions, and capped bars are propagated four-block
within-trajectory stability measures. Each composition and parameterization
has one 20 ns production trajectory; neither visual interval is independent-
replica uncertainty or a population confidence interval. The five-composition
means for I2, I3- and I2Br- are below iodide in both parameterizations. These
bulk, force-field-dependent calculations provide a bounded carrier-mobility
prior, not a porous-electrode effective diffusivity or validation of a
continuum transport constant.
