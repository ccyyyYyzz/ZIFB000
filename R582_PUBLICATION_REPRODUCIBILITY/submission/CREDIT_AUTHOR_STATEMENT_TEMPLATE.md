## Assignment safeguards

- Assign `Validation` only for validation work actually performed. Deterministic reruns, numerical convergence checks and reconstruction of existing data are not independent experimental validation.
- Assign `Funding acquisition` only to a contributor who secured verified support.
- Assign `Resources` only for a documented contribution of materials, instrumentation, computing access or other research resources.
- AI tools cannot receive authorship or CRediT roles.
- Every author must review and approve the final statement.
