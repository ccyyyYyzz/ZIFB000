## One-sentence message

The ZIFB positive electrode reaches average iodine saturation before calibrated accessibility loss, and the inventory-to-accessibility relation controls the later solid-inventory and voltage response.

## Three-block content

2. **Separated states:** show free-I2 supersaturation, retained solid-I2 inventory and remaining accessible area as distinct variables.
3. **Matched consequence:** show that changing only the accessibility relation leaves saturation nearly unchanged but changes the downstream voltage response.
