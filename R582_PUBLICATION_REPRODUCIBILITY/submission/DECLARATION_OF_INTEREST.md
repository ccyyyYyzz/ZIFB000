# Declaration of competing interests

Status: **human confirmation and Elsevier declaration-form export required before upload**.

All authors must disclose relevant financial and personal relationships to the corresponding author. The corresponding author must complete Elsevier’s declarations tool and upload the resulting `.doc` or `.docx` file. Do not upload this Markdown worksheet as the journal declaration.

## Route A — only after every author confirms that there is nothing to declare

Select “I have nothing to declare” in Elsevier’s declarations tool. The resulting form may use the following statement:

> The authors declare that they have no known competing financial interests or personal relationships that could have appeared to influence the work reported in this paper.

## Route B — if any author has a relevant interest

Enter the verified disclosure in Elsevier’s declarations tool. Check, at minimum, employment, consultancies, stock ownership, honoraria, paid expert testimony, patent applications or registrations, grants or other funding, and any editorial or advisory role with *Journal of Power Sources*.

Do not infer a no-conflict statement from the project files. Funding and competing-interest disclosures must be confirmed independently even when the same organization appears in both.
