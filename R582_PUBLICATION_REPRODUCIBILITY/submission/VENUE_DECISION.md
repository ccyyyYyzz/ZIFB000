# Venue decision — R582

Decision: target *Journal of Power Sources* as a Research Paper.

The manuscript addresses a porous zinc–iodine flow-battery positive electrode, with a state-resolved continuum model, existing full-cell records and bounded molecular/mesoscale calculations. This combination matches the journal’s coverage of redox flow batteries, component engineering and multiscale electrochemical modeling.

The principal venue risk is explicit: *Journal of Power Sources* prioritizes experimentally validated computational work, whereas the available full-cell records do not independently measure free-I2 supersaturation, retained-solid inventory or accessible area. The paper therefore presents those records as device-scale anchors and stress tests, not validation of internal positive-electrode states. The matched simulations establish controlled model-form sensitivity, not deposit morphology.

This venue choice does not broaden the evidence. No statement may convert EXP-META-001, NH4Br, full-cell voltage or continuum fields into support for a stronger mechanism claim.
