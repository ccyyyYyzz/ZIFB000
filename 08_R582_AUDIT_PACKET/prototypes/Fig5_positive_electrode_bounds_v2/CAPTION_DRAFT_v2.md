**Figure 5 | Independent bounds on positive-electrode model inputs.** **a,**
Periodic CP2K electronic energies for dry single-I2 placement, reported relative
to basal carbon. **b,** Five-SOC bulk-MD means for I-, I3- and I2Br- under
q = 0.8 ECC and formal-charge parameterizations in the representative 1 M ZnI2
+ 4 M NH4Br supporting electrolyte; error bars report propagated
within-trajectory stability. Shading denotes the formal-charge low-end carrier
proxy range (0.425-0.664 x 10^-9 m2 s^-1), and the dashed line marks the
continuum baseline D_eff = 0.50 x 10^-9 m2 s^-1. **c,** Remaining accessible
area for sparse (N = 10^11 m^-2) and dense (N = 10^14 m^-2) single-fibre
placement; lines connect computed nodes, including the exact zero-solid
boundary. **d,** Six-law permeability envelope for an idealized pore network
over the baseline solid-I2 range; the inset retains the full computed range. At
epsilon_s = 3.219 x 10^-3, K/K0 = 0.981-0.992. These calculations bound model
inputs and physical comparator families; they neither independently validate
the continuum outputs nor identify deposit morphology or pore closure.
